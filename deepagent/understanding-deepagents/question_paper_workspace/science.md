### **Answer Key & Explanations**

#### **41. Correct Answer: B (Speed)**
* **Explanation:** In uniform circular motion, a body travels along a circular path with a constant speed. However, its velocity changes continuously because its direction of motion is constantly changing at every point. Since velocity is changing, the motion is accelerated, meaning acceleration and displacement also change.

#### **42. Correct Answer: A ($+2\text{ D}$)**
* **Explanation:** The optical power $P$ of a lens is the reciprocal of its focal length $f$ in meters ($P = 1/f$).
  * $f = 50\text{ cm} = 0.5\text{ m}$
  * $P = 1 / 0.5\text{ m} = +2\text{ Dioptres (D)}$
  * A convex lens has a positive focal length, so its power is positive ($+2\text{ D}$).

#### **43. Correct Answer: B (Fluorine)**
* **Explanation:** Electronegativity is the tendency of an atom to attract a shared pair of electrons. It increases across a period from left to right and decreases down a group. Fluorine (F) is located at the top-right of the reactive non-metals in Group 17, making it the most electronegative element in the entire periodic table, with a value of $3.98$ on the Pauling scale.

#### **44. Correct Answer: B (Calcium hydroxide; Exothermic combination)**
* **Explanation:** When quicklime ($\text{CaO}$) reacts with water ($\text{H}_2\text{O}$), it forms slaked lime [calcium hydroxide, $\text{Ca(OH)}_2$]. The reaction is highly exothermic because it releases a large amount of heat energy, and it is a combination reaction as two reactants combine to form a single product:
  $$\text{CaO (s)} + \text{H}_2\text{O (l)} \rightarrow \text{Ca(OH)}_2\text{ (aq)} + \text{Heat}$$

#### **45. Correct Answer: B (Aluminum)**
* **Explanation:** Aluminum ($\text{Al}$) is the most abundant metallic element in the Earth's crust, accounting for about $8.1\%$ of its total weight. Overall, it is the third most abundant element in the crust, after oxygen (most abundant non-metal) and silicon (metalloid). Iron is the second most abundant metal.

#### **46. Correct Answer: C (Mitochondria)**
* **Explanation:** Mitochondria are known as the "powerhouses of the cell" because they contain the enzymes necessary for aerobic respiration. They oxidize carbohydrates and fats to generate chemical energy stored in the form of ATP (Adenosine Triphosphate), which the cell uses for various metabolic activities.

#### **47. Correct Answer: C (Insulin)**
* **Explanation:** Insulin is a peptide hormone produced and secreted by the beta cells of the pancreatic islets (Islets of Langerhans). It plays a vital role in metabolism, primarily by facilitating the uptake of glucose into cells and lowering blood glucose levels.

#### **48. Correct Answer: B (Xylem)**
* **Explanation:** Xylem is a complex, specialized vascular tissue in plants responsible for the unidirectional conduction of water and dissolved inorganic mineral nutrients from the roots to the aerial parts (stems and leaves). Phloem, on the other hand, is responsible for the bidirectional transport of organic nutrients (food/sucrose) synthesized during photosynthesis.

#### **49. Correct Answer: B ($1\ \Omega$)**
* **Explanation:** For a parallel combination of resistors, the reciprocal of the equivalent resistance ($R_p$) is the sum of the reciprocals of the individual resistances:
  $$\frac{1}{R_p} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \frac{1}{R_3}$$
  $$\frac{1}{R_p} = \frac{1}{2} + \frac{1}{3} + \frac{1}{6}$$
  $$\frac{1}{R_p} = \frac{3 + 2 + 1}{6} = \frac{6}{6} = 1\ \Omega^{-1}$$
  $$R_p = 1\ \Omega$$

#### **50. Correct Answer: C (Hemoglobin)**
* **Explanation:** Hemoglobin is a complex, iron-containing metalloprotein present in the red blood cells (erythrocytes) of humans and other vertebrates. It binds reversibly to oxygen in the lungs and transports it to tissues throughout the body, while also giving blood its characteristic red color.
