#### **Question 10 (Ranking & Ordering)**
Among five friends — P, Q, R, S, and T — each has a different height.

B) Q
C) R
D) S

### **Answer Key & Explanations**

#### **1. Correct Answer: C (272)**
* **Explanation:** The logic follows the pattern: $x : x^2 + x$.
  * For the first term: $14 : 14^2 + 14 = 196 + 14 = 210$.
  * Applying the same logic to the third term: $16 : 16^2 + 16 = 256 + 16 = 272$.

#### **2. Correct Answer: B (PRUX)**
* **Explanation:** Find the letter gaps (alphabetic position change) for each cluster:
  * **JLOQ**: J (+2) $\rightarrow$ L (+3) $\rightarrow$ O (+2) $\rightarrow$ Q. (Pattern: +2, +3, +2)
  * **PRUX**: P (+2) $\rightarrow$ R (+3) $\rightarrow$ U (+3) $\rightarrow$ X. (Pattern: +2, +3, +3)
  * **WYBD**: W (+2) $\rightarrow$ Y (+3) $\rightarrow$ B (+2) $\rightarrow$ D. (Pattern: +2, +3, +2)
  * **KMPR**: K (+2) $\rightarrow$ M (+3) $\rightarrow$ P (+2) $\rightarrow$ R. (Pattern: +2, +3, +2)
  * Cluster **PRUX** is the odd one because it follows a (+2, +3, +3) pattern instead of (+2, +3, +2).

#### **3. Correct Answer: A (VMRGEYN)**
* **Explanation:** The logic is shifting forward and backward by 2 alternately: odd-positioned letters increase by 2 (+2) and even-positioned letters decrease by 2 (-2):
  * G (+2) $\rightarrow$ I; L (-2) $\rightarrow$ J; A (+2) $\rightarrow$ C; M (-2) $\rightarrow$ K; O (+2) $\rightarrow$ Q; U (-2) $\rightarrow$ S; R (+2) $\rightarrow$ T.
  * Applying this pattern to **TOPICAL**:
    * T (+2) $\rightarrow$ **V**
    * O (-2) $\rightarrow$ **M**
    * P (+2) $\rightarrow$ **R**
    * I (-2) $\rightarrow$ **G**
    * C (+2) $\rightarrow$ **E**
    * A (-2) $\rightarrow$ **Y**
    * L (+2) $\rightarrow$ **N**
  * Thus, the code is **VMRGEYN**.

#### **4. Correct Answer: C (566)**
* **Explanation:** The pattern of the series is:
  * $4 \times 2 + 1 = 9$
  * $9 \times 3 + 1 = 28$
  * $28 \times 4 + 1 = 113$
  * Following the pattern: $113 \times 5 + 1 = 565 + 1 = 566$.

#### **5. Correct Answer: B (Father)**
* **Explanation:** Let's break down the statement made by the man:
  * "My mother-in-law's only daughter" $\rightarrow$ The man's wife.
  * "Her [the woman's] mother is [the man's wife]" $\rightarrow$ The woman is the daughter of the man's wife.
  * Therefore, the man is the **Father** of the woman.

#### **6. Correct Answer: B (17 meters, South-East)**
* **Explanation:** Let's plot Rahul's movements from his house at coordinates $(0, 0)$:
  * Walks 12 meters East $\rightarrow (12, 0)$
  * Turns left (North) and walks 10 meters $\rightarrow (12, 10)$
  * Turns right (East) and walks 3 meters $\rightarrow (15, 10)$
  * Turns right (South) and walks 18 meters $\rightarrow (15, 10 - 18) = (15, -8)$
  * To find the shortest distance from starting point $(0,0)$ to ending point $(15, -8)$:
    $$\text{Distance} = \sqrt{15^2 + (-8)^2} = \sqrt{225 + 64} = \sqrt{289} = 17 \text{ meters}$$
  * Direction: Position $(15, -8)$ lies East and South of $(0, 0)$, which is **South-East**.

#### **7. Correct Answer: A (Only conclusion I follows)**
* **Explanation:**
  * Since some doctors are lawyers, and all lawyers are engineers, the portion of doctors who are lawyers must also be engineers. Thus, **Conclusion I follows**.
  * Since some doctors are lawyers, it means some lawyers are indeed doctors. This directly contradicts "No lawyer is a doctor". Thus, **Conclusion II does not follow**.

#### **8. Correct Answer: B (16)**
* **Explanation:** Substitute the mathematical operators as specified:
  * '+' $\rightarrow$ '$\div$'
  * '-' $\rightarrow$ '$\times$'
  * '$\times$' $\rightarrow$ '-'
  * '$\div$' $\rightarrow$ '+'
  * The expression becomes: $36 \div 6 \times 3 - 5 + 3$
  * Apply BODMAS rules:
    1. Division: $36 \div 6 = 6 \rightarrow 6 \times 3 - 5 + 3$
    2. Multiplication: $6 \times 3 = 18 \rightarrow 18 - 5 + 3$
    3. Addition & Subtraction: $18 - 5 + 3 = 13 + 3 = 16$.

#### **9. Correct Answer: A (One large circle containing another circle, which in turn contains a third circle)**
* **Explanation:** 
  * All cities are located within states, so the 'City' circle must be entirely enclosed by the 'State' circle.
  * All states are located within a country, so the 'State' circle must be entirely enclosed by the 'Country' circle.
  * This forms three concentric circles.

#### **10. Correct Answer: B (Q)**
* **Explanation:** Arrange the heights in descending order:
  * "P is taller than only S" $\rightarrow \_ > \_ > \_ > P > S$ (S is the shortest).
  * "R is shorter than only T" $\rightarrow T > R > \_ > P > S$ (T is the tallest).
  * "Q is taller than P but shorter than R" $\rightarrow$ Q fits the remaining middle slot: $T > R > Q > P > S$.
  * Comparing heights from tallest to shortest: 1st tallest is T, 2nd is R, and 3rd is **Q**.
