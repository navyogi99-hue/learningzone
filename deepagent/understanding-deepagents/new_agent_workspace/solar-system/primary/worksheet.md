## Activity 1: Match the Planet to its Clue!

Draw a line from each planet on the left to its correct clue on the right.

| Planet | | Clue |
| :--- | :---: | :--- |
| **1. Mercury** | | A) I am the largest planet and have a giant red storm spot. |
| **2. Venus** | | B) I am called the "Red Planet" because of my rusty soil. |
| **3. Earth** | | C) I am the closest planet to the Sun and move very fast. |
| **4. Mars** | | D) I have spectacular, wide rings made of ice and dust. |
| **5. Jupiter** | | E) I spin completely on my side like a bowling ball. |
| **6. Saturn** | | F) I am the hottest planet because of my thick gas clouds. |
| **7. Uranus** | | G) I am the farthest planet from the Sun and very windy. |
| **8. Neptune** | | H) I am the only planet known to support living things. |

## Answer Key for Parents & Teachers

### Activity 1: Matching
* 1 -> **C** (Mercury is closest and fast)
* 2 -> **F** (Venus is the hottest because of thick clouds)
* 3 -> **H** (Earth supports life)
* 4 -> **B** (Mars is the Red Planet)
* 5 -> **A** (Jupiter is the largest with a giant storm)
* 6 -> **D** (Saturn has spectacular rings)
* 7 -> **E** (Uranus spins on its side)
* 8 -> **G** (Neptune is the farthest and windy)

### Activity 2: Word Scramble
1. **SUN**
2. **ORBIT**
3. **COMET**
4. **GRAVITY**

### Activity 3: True or False
1. **False** (All four outer gas giants have rings, but Saturn's are just the brightest and most famous).
2. **True** (The Sun is a medium-sized star).
3. **False** (Jupiter is a gas giant and has no solid ground to walk on).
4. **True** (Mars has Olympus Mons, the largest volcano in the Solar System).
5. **False** (The Moon does not make its own light; it reflects light from the Sun).
