## Purpose

Create an engaging LinkedIn post that educates, inspires, shares experiences, or promotes professional insights while encouraging interaction.

## Inputs

* Topic
* Objective (Educate, Share Experience, Announce, Promote, Inspire)
* Target audience
* Desired tone (Professional, Conversational, Thought Leadership, Inspirational)
* Call to action (optional)

## Output

A LinkedIn-ready post in Markdown format.

---

## Instructions

### 1. Understand the Goal

Determine the primary purpose:

* Educate the audience
* Share a personal learning
* Announce an achievement
* Discuss industry trends
* Promote a project or product
* Start a conversation

### 2. Create a Strong Hook

The first 1-2 lines should make people stop scrolling.

Examples:

* Most developers are learning AI the wrong way.
* I spent 6 months learning Docker. Here's what I'd do differently.
* One mistake cost us an entire day of production downtime.

### 3. Write the Main Content

Structure:

1. Hook
2. Context or story
3. Key insight(s)
4. Actionable takeaway

Guidelines:

* Use short paragraphs.
* Use simple language.
* Focus on one core message.
* Include personal experience when possible.
* Avoid excessive jargon.

### 4. Add Engagement Elements

Use:

* Bullet points
* Numbered lists
* Lessons learned
* Questions

Examples:

* What has been your experience?
* Do you agree?
* What would you add?

### 5. End with a Call to Action

Examples:

* What's your opinion?
* Have you faced something similar?
* Share your experience below.

### 6. Hashtag Guidelines

Use 3-5 relevant hashtags.

Example:

```text
#AI #Python #Docker #SoftwareEngineering #Learning
```

---

## LinkedIn Post Template

```text
[Hook]

[Story or Context]

[Key Lessons / Insights]

1. Insight one
2. Insight two
3. Insight three

[Takeaway]

[Question or CTA]

#RelevantHashtags
```

---

## Quality Checklist

Before publishing:

* Strong opening hook
* Single clear message
* Easy to skim
* Actionable insight
* Professional tone
* Encourages engagement
* 3-5 relevant hashtags
* No unnecessary emojis

## Example Usage

Input:

```yaml
topic: Learning Docker
objective: Educate
audience: Software Developers
tone: Conversational
```

Expected Output:

A LinkedIn post sharing lessons learned while learning Docker, practical tips, a takeaway, and a question inviting discussion.
